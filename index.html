About Me

Hi! I'm Isaac. My interest in technology/coding began in high school when I built my first application using Python and a Raspberry Pi. My interest for software creation and problem-solving was ignited by that experience, and it has grown throughout both my professional and academic life. I completed a Bachelor of Information Technology at RMIT University, where I developed a strong foundation in software engineering, data structures, database systems and web development. Through various projects, I gained hands-on experience designing, building, and deploying software solutions while working both independently and in team environments.

I'm now in the final stages of my Master of Artificial Intelligence, deepening my expertise in machine learning, deep learning, and intelligent systems. I enjoy solving real-world problems through code - from building scalable web applications to designing AI-powered solutions - and I'm actively seeking graduate opportunities where I can contribute and grow.
